The latency analysis under increasing traffic rates highlights the system’s responsiveness under high load conditions. The baseline system shows a rapid increase in delay as traffic intensity rises, primarily due to congestion and queuing delays.

The proposed system maintains lower latency levels by processing data at the edge and prioritizing critical traffic. This reduces the load on the core network and ensures faster packet delivery.

The results demonstrate that edge computing and traffic prioritization are effective strategies for controlling latency. This is essential for applications requiring real-time communication.
